About Techstars Startup Weekend Dhamtari

What is Startup Weekend?

Techstars Startup Weekend is a three-day immersive program where people come together to share ideas, form teams, and experience what it’s like to build a startup. The format is simple: Learn, Design, Build, and Pitch. By the end of the weekend, people present their Hardware or Software solution with opportunities to connect for support and potential funding.

Why Dhamtari?

For the first time, Startup Weekend is coming to Dhamtari, giving local students, working professionals, and startup founders the opportunity to collaborate and learn the startup way of building in just 54 hours.

What to Expect?

  • Networking: Connect with like-minded participants, potential co-founders, and industry peers.
  • Mentorship & Guidance: Learn from experienced mentors, industry experts, and successful startup founders.
  • Interactive Activites: Participate in team challenges, brainstroming sessions, and real-world problem solving.
  • Funding Opportunities: Showcase your solution in front of 10+ investors and explore avenues for support and funding from investors.

Who Can Join?

Students, working professionals and startup founders those who are in early stage or simply those who are curious about startups and entrepreneurship.

The official dates for the Techstars Startup Weekend are 28th, 29th, and 30th November 2025.

The event is intended to be a collaborative and inclusive forum for sharing, learning, building, and having fun. We expect each member of the startup community to be inclusive of all people and their ideas at Techstars Startup Weekend Dhamtari and we welcome all, regardless of experience, industry or background.

Apart from the organizing team, mentors, speakers, event volunteers, and judges, everyone who participates in our Techstars Startup Weekend Dhamtari is expected to participate in all three days. This is important not only to preserve your own learning experience, but also to minimize distractions or disruptions for the team you choose to join.

At our event, a “Sunday Presentation Only” option may be available for those wishing to only view the teams’ final pitch presentations. We welcome any friends, family, or colleagues you would like to invite to watch your final presentation. Please check our registration page for availability.

To register for our Techstars Startup Weekend Dhamtari, please visit swdhamtari.com where you’ll find the registration link. We offer different ticket types based on skill sets. Business (strategy, marketing, operations, and pitching), Developer (coders and engineers), Designer (UI/UX), and Enthusiast (students or anyone exploring startups).

Our organizing team uses this system to make sure we have a diverse mix of participants. Just pick the ticket that’s closest to your background. What matters is your enthusiasm to learn, build, and collaborate!

Pitching is open to every participant! We recommend you purchase the ticket that best represents your skill set.

Everyone who attends the event as a participant is expected to come without a team and then join one during the event. The ideal team consists of diverse members collaborating to build a minimum viable product (MVP) using the Lean Canvas framework. More people = faster MVP development and a stronger final pitch.
